  • A Bit About Us

    Our mission is simple, we're just three Physicists (and an Engineer!) looking to share our passion of STEM! Made to commemerate Women's History Month, STEM resourced is an easy, efficient and elegant way of bringing together the various STEM disciplines. So whether you're an Electronics enthusiast looking for inspiration or a keen Chemist looking to score the top grades, this is the place for you!
